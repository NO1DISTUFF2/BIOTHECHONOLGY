Job Description

Biotechnology is the science of using living things, like plants, animals, and tiny organisms, to create useful products and solve problems. Biotechnologists work in labs and they go outside to collect samples from nature, like soil, water, plants, or animals.

In the field, they might study how plants grow in different conditions, check crops for diseases, or observe animals to help protect them. They use special tools to gather data, write down their observations carefully, and share what they find with other scientists. This work helps improve medicine, farming, and the environment.
